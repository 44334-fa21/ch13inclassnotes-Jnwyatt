Components of a Search Engine

Robot

A robot is a program that automatically traverses the hypertext structure of the web by retrieving a web page document and following the hyperlinks on the page. In addtion, some robots may access and store title , meta tag descriptions, and some text on the page.

Database

THe collection of information that is organized so that its contents can be accessed, managed and updated. The web page that displays the results of your search has information from the database that is used by the search engine site. Some search engines some of the content recieved by other search engines. Such as Yahoo! Which gets its primary search Results from Google.

Search Engine Optimization

Keywords

Spend time brainstorming terms and phrases that people may use when searching for your site. These terms are what describe your website or business.

Page Title

A descriptive title which includes your company name or website name will help your site market itself. The page title is often displayed in the SERP and saved when a visitor bookmarks your page. Do not use the exact same title for every page on your website, include keywords in your page title thart are appropriate for your site.

Heading Tags

Use structured tags, h1, h2,ect to organize your content. If appropriate include some keywordsin the text contained within heading tags. Some search engines will give higher list postion if keywords are used in the page title or heading. However, do ot spam keywords, meaning repeating the words over and over within content of the page. Search engines continue to be more sophisticated and spamming can prevent your page from being listed if it percieves you are trying to cheat the system.

Descriptions

Write a few sentences about your website or business that makes it special and why people would want to visit. The description should be inviting and intersesting so that someone would choose your site.

Description Meta Tag

A self- contained tag that is placed in the head section of a web page and is displayed on the SERP by some search engines, such as Google.

Linking

Verify that all hyperlinks are working. Each page on your website should be reachable such as moreinfo or click here. Should use keywords as apppropriate.

Images and Multimedia

Search engine robots do not see text within your images. Configure meaningfult alt text for images and include releveant keywords.

Vaild Code

While it is not required by search engines that your code be validated, well structured and vaild code is more easliy processed by search engine robots which could help your placement in the search engine.

Content of Value

Your Website should contain high- quality content thati s of value to visitors.

Link Popularity

Search engines rate websites based of the number of sites that link to a particular website and the quality of those sites. Link popularity can determine its order in the SERP.
